1. The Service

Node is an iOS app that lets you create and join private invite-only groups (called "nodes") of 2 to 10 people. Within each node you can share photos, stories, and short text posts with the other members.

You must be at least 13 years old to use Node. By creating an account you confirm you are at least 13. If you are under the age of majority in your jurisdiction, you must have permission from a parent or guardian.

3. Your account

Node uses Sign in with Apple. You are responsible for keeping your Apple ID secure. You may delete your account at any time from the in-app Settings; this deletes your account, your posts, and your media.

4. Acceptable use

You agree not to use Node to post or share:

  • Spam, scams, malware, or any content used to commit fraud
  • Personal information of others without their consent

Violation of these rules may result in immediate removal of content, suspension of your account, and / or removal from one or more nodes by the node owner or by Node staff.

5. Reporting and moderation

Every story, photo, thought, member, and node has a Report option. Reports go to Node support. Action taken may include content removal, user blocking, or account termination.
