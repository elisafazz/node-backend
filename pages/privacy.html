Node -- Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-04

This document is a placeholder draft. A finalized Privacy Policy will be published before App Store submission.

What we collect

  • Account identifiers: an opaque Apple user identifier from Sign in with Apple, plus your chosen display name and optional avatar
  • Content you post: photos, stories, captions, text posts (called "thoughts"), per-node identity overrides
  • Device data: APNs device token (so we can send push notifications), iOS version, app version
  • Diagnostics: error logs (no personal content) for debugging

We do NOT collect: email address, real name, phone number, contacts, location, advertising identifiers, or analytics events.

What we don't collect

We do not have user-tracking analytics. We do not sell or share data with advertisers. We do not buy data about you from anyone.

Where data is stored

  • Database: Supabase (Postgres on AWS, region TBD)
  • Media (photos and story images): Cloudinary
  • Push delivery: Apple Push Notification service

Who can see your content

Only the members of the specific node where you posted can see your content. We use Postgres row-level security to enforce this on every query. Outside that node, your content is invisible -- including to Node staff, except when reviewing a report or fulfilling an account deletion request.

Account deletion

You may delete your account from in-app Settings. Deletion:

  • Revokes the Apple authorization grant via Apple's `/auth/revoke` endpoint
  • Deletes all content you authored (stories, photos, thoughts) from our database
  • Deletes all media you uploaded from Cloudinary
  • Removes you from every node you were a member of
  • Deletes your user record

Deletion is permanent and irreversible. We retain a deletion-request audit row for compliance purposes.

Children

Node is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated in-app.
